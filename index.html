Tulip

What is the Tulip Project?

The Tulip Project is a student-led nonprofit organization dedicated to destigmatizing and spreading awareness about female puberty and menstrual health. Through our mobile app, Tulip, and various community events, we strive to educate and empower women around the world.

 

Why is education about female puberty important?

Around the world girls face a lack of information about their own body development as well as negative stigmas and misinformation which can be dangerous and unhealthy. Because of this, it is important to educate everyone about female puberty in order to keep girls safe, healthy, and happy.

Where is the Tulip Project located?


The Tulip Project is an international organization primarily based in Houston, Texas, USA.
