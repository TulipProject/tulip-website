What is the Tulip Project?


The Tulip Project is a nonprofit organization dedicated to de-stigmatizing and spreading awareness about female puberty and menstrual health. Through our mobile app, Tulip, and various community events, we hope to educate and empower women around the world.




FAQ's




In addition to our mobile app that provides educational resources for young girls, we also hold menstrual product drives, workshops, and other community events.


The Tulip Project is an international organization primarily based in Katy, Texas, USA.


Around the world girls face a lack of information about their own body development as well as negative stigmas and misinformation which can be dangerous and unhealthy. Because of this, it is important to educate everyone about female puberty in order to keep girls safe, healthy, and happy.
